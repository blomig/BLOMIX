BLOMIX — Rules

These are the rules :
- Stack blox in the columns.
- Form chains of at least 5 cells of the same colour (8-direction neighbourhood) to clear them and score points.
- Prix (special blox with a number on it) need several chain hits nearby before they disappear.
- Every 10 moves, a new line rises from the bottom.

You can get rid of a zone (included Prix) with bombs.
Enjoy !
